About Us

Founded in 2018, Green Earth Initiative is a nonprofit organization committed to environmental conservation. We work with communities to reduce waste, plant trees, and promote renewable energy. Our mission is to create a sustainable world for future generations through education and action.

We've planted over 10,000 trees and partnered with schools for eco-workshops. Join our efforts today!
